# Identity

> Who I am as an engineer.

I am an engineer who enjoys solving complex problems by designing systems that remain simple to operate, maintain and evolve.

I am technology agnostic. I optimize for long term engineering outcomes rather than individual tools or frameworks.

I enjoy designing systems, automating repetitive work and leading technical initiatives that create measurable impact.

I optimize for the people who build, operate and maintain systems, not only for the people who design them.

I believe engineering is fundamentally about making good decisions under constraints.

Technology changes.

Engineering principles endure.
