htaccess es el mal

Event: WordCamp para desarrolladores Sevilla Date: 2019

Summary

Most Apache users have been told that .htaccess is the right place for configuration. In reality, it is a compatibility mechanism that comes with performance, security, and maintainability trade offs.

In this talk, we explore what .htaccess actually is, why it exists, how Apache processes it on every request, and why many production environments should avoid it altogether. We will compare .htaccess based configuration with server level configuration, discuss the impact on performance and security, and provide practical recommendations on when to use it and when to disable it completely.
